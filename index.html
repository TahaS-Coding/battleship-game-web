Traditional Battleship with 3 Unique Twists

  1. Ships are always moving/rotating depending on movement speed
  2. Both player will no know where their ships are
  3. Each ships has its own unique ability

How to play

  1. Player(s) need to select 5 ships
  2. Click on ability to activate ability. Click again to deactivate ability.
  3. Aircraft Carrier ability hits in a 3x3 area
  4. Cruiser doubles the damage
  5. Destroyer hits in a vertical line
  6. Battleship hits in a horizontal line
  7. Frigate finds location of a random ship
